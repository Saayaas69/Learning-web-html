Allison Becker

Álisson Ramsés Becker (born 2 October 1992), known as Alisson Becker or simply Alisson, is a Brazilian professional footballer who plays as a goalkeeper for Premier League club Liverpool and the Brazil national team. Regarded as one of the best goalkeepers of his generation,[4] he is renowned for his immense shot stopping, distribution and ability in one-on-one situations.
